Downtowns to Visit in New Jersey (My Home State)

If you're a New Jersey resident, or visiting, you're lucky ...

I've compiled a few of my favorite New Jersey downtowns that I strongly recommend you visit. Each one of these locations exhibit a fun atmosphere with snacks shops and restaurants scattered around, guaranteeing you and your partner a whole day's worth of enjoyment and relaxation. Let me know in the review tab if you would like more recommendations!

1. Westfield

Here is a downtown that I find myself returning to time and time again. Downtown Westfield is a perfect place to take a date, either it be to make a good first impression or to keep the sparks going with your long-time lover. You can have fun in an arcade that has it all, walk around during sunset to admire the colorful buildings around, and wrap up your night with a dinner that won't disappoint. My personal favorites for a top rating dining experience would be Spice Bazaar, an Indian cuisine restaurant with the friendliest staff and the best of food, or Sweet Waters Steakhouse, a classy restaurant famous for their, well, steaks, although I will say their french onion soup is a must try. As for the arcade that has it all, Westfield's Yestercades is a favorite for people of all ages, with its wide variety of retro arcade games as well as pool tables and more. This arcade is guaranteed to keep you and your partner entertained, or competing.

2. Summit

Downtown Summit is a sweet little downtown that some people may dismiss, but it certainly is never empty at peak hours. All around this downtown are lights set up around stores and lamp posts to give an almost cozy Christmas feeling when walking around with your partner. Here is where I would go for a date to really connect with my partner by spending some solid quality time with them. McCool's Ice Cream is a very small, but very tasty shop that will never leave your eyes or tastebuds dissatisfied because of its wide variety of flavors and creative colors. Now to wind down and enjoy your ice cream, you can head over to Scotti's Record Shop and browse this vintage style shop that sells all kinds of vinyls, from jazz to hip hop and more. Now, two of my favorite places that I can rely on for a good bite with a good vibe would be Roots Steakhouse, an upscale restaurant where you will always leave with a happy tummy (even if your wallet may not be), and Summit Diner, a cute little diner perfect for a casual bite at any time of the day.

3. Jersey City

Just taking a day to walk around Jersey City is good enough as a date because of its perfect placement for sunset pictures of the sky. While that may just be my own opinion, a nice sunset can really elevate the romance of any date night, especially if the rest of your day is filled with all the fun activities available in Jersey City. Aside from the vast amount of street art that fills this downtown, there are also sometimes fairs and festivals that are sure to hit at least one interest. Downtown Jersey City has got to be one of my favorites when it comes to casual snacking. One of my favorite spots to go to is Bang Cookies, where they sell some of the best cookies either of us have ever had, while simultaneously using only organic ingredients. As for a romantic dining experience, head on over to Skinner's Loft, where they sometimes have live jazz to accompany the most intimate ambience for dining. For anyone that might be interested in thrifting clothes or finding vintage gems, make sure to stop at Another Man's Treasure. It has the perfect atmosphere for an avid (or beginner) thrifter and will guarantee you leave with at least an item.
